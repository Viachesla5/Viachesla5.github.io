FORM Swim Goggles

Smart swim goggles

If you think paying more than £100 for a pair of swimming goggles is a bit steep, you'll be pleased to know there's a reason for the hefty price tag. These are smart goggles, so you can view metrics and workout instructions on the lens as you swim. You can view your swim time, distance and pace, so they're a great idea if you're sick of counting laps. After syncing up with an app you can track your performance and customize your goggles.
